Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI Text Detector 100% accurate?

Our AI model has a 98% accuracy rate, but no system is perfect. We cross-reference multiple patterns and data points to provide the most reliable score possible.

What are the free usage limits?

Currently, you can analyze up to 50 news articles and convert up to 5,000 words per day for free. Premium plans will be introduced later.

How does the Rumor Tracker get data?

We use a combination of open-source intelligence (OSINT), social media APIs, and natural language processing to detect when and where a specific narrative is spreading.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not store the text you paste into the converter. Analyzed news URLs are logged to improve our public database, but personal data is never shared.

Can I integrate AIBrain via API?

We are currently beta-testing our API for enterprise partners. Please select "Partnership" in the contact form to request early access.
